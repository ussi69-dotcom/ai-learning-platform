## ğŸ§± 4. Technical Skill: Delimiters

This is a technique used by Master Engineers. When you paste a long text for the AI to analyze, the AI can get confused about where your *instructions* end and the *data* begins.

We use **Delimiters** (separators) to draw a clear line.

### Common Delimiters:
*   `"""` (Triple Quotes) - Best for multi-line text.
*   `---` (Dashes) - Good for separating sections.
*   `###` (Hashes) - Good for headers.
*   `<text>` (XML Tags) - Good for strict structure.

<ConceptCard title="Why use Delimiters?" icon="ğŸ›¡ï¸">
*   **Clarity:** Helps the AI distinguish between YOUR words and the TEXT'S words.
*   **Security:** Prevents "Prompt Injection" (where the text tries to hack the AI).
</ConceptCard>
