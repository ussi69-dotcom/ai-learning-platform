## ğŸ§  9. Chain of Thought (Logic Hack)

If the AI fails at math or logic, it is "rushing".
Force it to slow down with the magic phrase: **"Let's think step by step."**

<Diagram type="chain-of-thought" />

### ğŸ”¬ Lab 5: The Reasoning Anchor

**Objective:** Force the AI to handle complex constraints that overwhelm its short-term memory.

**The Prompt:**
```text
You are planning a workshop schedule.
Constraint 1: We have 3 rooms (Red, Blue, Green).
Constraint 2: Each room hosts max 2 sessions.
Constraint 3: "Session A" shares an instructor with "Session C" (they cannot be at the same time).
Constraint 4: "Session B" needs the Projector (only in Red Room).

Create a valid schedule for Sessions A, B, C, D, E, F.
Let's think step by step.
```

**Analysis:**
*   **Without CoT:** The AI often hallucinates a schedule where Session A and C clash, or puts Session B in the Blue room. It tries to "guess" the answer in one go.
*   **With CoT:** The magic phrase forces it to write: *"First, let's place Session B in Red. Next, let's schedule A and C in different slots..."* This "scratchpad" thinking ensures accuracy.
</LabSection>
